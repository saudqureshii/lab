It is recommended to fast for at least 8-12 hours before certain blood tests, such as glucose and lipid panel tests. Here are some general guidelines to follow before your blood test:
  • Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated.
  • Avoid alcohol and heavy meals 24 hours prior to the test.
  • Inform your doctor about any medications you are taking, as some may affect test results.
  • Wear loose-fitting clothing for easier access to your arm.
Always follow your doctor’s specific advice regarding preparation for your test.
